Hi, I'm Luke. For most of my career I worked in operations at Anglian Water — managing teams, delivering projects, and spending a lot of time working alongside technology that was changing how water companies serve their customers. Logic, reasoning and problem solving are what I gravitate towards. Customer-centric thinking and stakeholder engagement come naturally to me.

Eventually I decided to stop working alongside the technology and start building it myself. I'm currently studying full stack web development at Code Institute and finding that a lot of what I learned managing complex projects and leading teams translates surprisingly well to writing code.

I am currently based in Cambridgeshire but open to relocation for the right opportunity.
